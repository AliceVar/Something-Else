Authentically Delicious

From the Coast of Greece to the Mountains of Jasper

Something Else Steakhouse brings warm Greek hospitality, fresh ingredients, and comforting family recipes to the heart of Jasper. Our food is inspired by traditional Mediterranean flavors and the welcoming mountain lifestyle.

Whether you are joining us for steak, pizza, pasta, seafood, or a special dinner with family, we want every guest to feel at home.
